# Agile Engineering - Go_TOFU

## What Problem We Are Solving

- Real user interviews are slow, expensive, and hard to scale.
- Teams cannot access enough customers to talk to, especially in early stages.
- Existing customer data (surveys, reviews, transcripts) is often unused.
- Getting expert or diverse opinions takes significant time and resources.

## Approach

- Option A: Synthetic responses
- Option B: Pre-personas based on publicly available data
- Option C: Proprietary data from customer data

## Data Sources for Scraping

1. App stores
   - AppStore (Apple)
   - PlayStore (Google)
   - ProductHunt
   - G2
   - Capterra
2. Social media
   - Reddit
   - Twitter/X
   - Instagram
   - Facebook
   - YouTube
3. E-commerce
   - Amazon, Shopify, AliExpress, Etsy, Ebay

## Who It Is For

- Founders validating ideas without enough interviews
- Marketers and researchers needing fast qualitative feedback
- Teams handling large amounts of customer feedback without clear insights

## Solution

- AI-powered synthetic personas built from real and public data
- Users submit goals and data to simulate customer or expert interviews
- The product turns broad feedback into decision-ready insights quickly
