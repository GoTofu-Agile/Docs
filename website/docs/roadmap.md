# Roadmap

## Vision

Enable teams to run high-quality synthetic interviews powered by real customer context.

## 90-Day Goals

- Validate top 2 customer segments.
- Ship MVP interview simulation flow.
- Launch first external beta with real feedback loops.

## Current Sprint Focus

- Finalize data source priorities.
- Define core interview output format.
- Align MVP scope with engineering constraints.

## Milestones

| Milestone | Owner | Target Date | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Problem/Solution Fit | Team | 2026-04-15 | In progress |
| MVP Build Complete | Engineering | 2026-05-15 | Planned |
| Beta Launch | Product | 2026-06-01 | Planned |

## Risks

- Limited interview volume from target personas.
- Data quality inconsistency across sources.
- Scope growth before MVP completion.
