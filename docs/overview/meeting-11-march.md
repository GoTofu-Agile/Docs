## 2️⃣ Pivot Alignment (10 min)

**Product Owner explains (max. 5 min)**

- What is the new core problem we are solving?
- Who is our target user?
- What is our core value proposition?

**Team Alignment Check (5 min)**

**Question to the team:**

“Can everyone explain in one sentence what we are building now?”

If there is confusion → clarify immediately.

Do not move forward without alignment.

## 3️⃣ Lock the MVP Scope (15 min)

**✅ Must Have – by End of March**

- Data upload (text / CSV)
- Clustering → 2–3 personas
- Synthetic interview engine
- Structured insight output

**❌ Not Part of the MVP**

- Analytics dashboard
- CRM / enterprise integrations
- Overengineered authentication
- Fancy visualizations
- “Nice-to-have” experiments

**Scope Test**

“If we remove this, is the product still functional?”

If **yes** → cut it.

---

## 4️⃣ Define Sprint 1 Goal (10 min)

The Sprint Goal must be:

- One clear sentence
- Measurable and concrete
- Not “we work on everything”
- Fully committed by the team

**Example:**

“We can ingest raw data and generate stable, reproducible persona clusters from it.”

---

## 5️⃣ Ownership & Blockers (5 min)

Each team member briefly answers:

1. What am I working on in Sprint 1?
2. What do I need to get started?
3. What could potentially block me?

Possible ownership areas:

- Data ingestion
- Clustering & persona modeling
- Simulation engine
- Insight structuring
- Basic frontend flow

# ✅ Definition of Success (Meeting)

- No one is still debating scope
- Everyone knows what they are working on
- The Sprint Goal is clearly formulated
- No fundamental strategic confusion remains
