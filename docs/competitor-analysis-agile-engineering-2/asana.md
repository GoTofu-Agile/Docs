# Asana

1. Question: Solves cross-team work coordination: projects/tasks, workflow automation, and tying work to goals/reporting; positioned for organizations and distributed teams managing initiatives at scale.
2. Question: No. Positioned as a general work management platform (projects, workflows, goals) for many functions, not a PM-only product.
3. Question: Goal-to-work hierarchy (“projects and portfolios ladder to objectives”); resource/workload visibility; standardized intake via forms; rules + AI-assisted workflow automation; timeline & Gantt-style planning views.
Description: Asana is a cloud-based work management platform designed to help teams organize, track, and manage projects, tasks, and workflows. It centralizes communication and collaboration, allowing users to visualize work through lists, boards, and timelines to meet deadlines and achieve goals.
Notes:   • More of an all purpose platform
  • No specific focus on product management
  • More focused on general company workflows
  •
PM: No
Solutions: Admin & Security, Goals & Reporting, IT, Leaders, Marketing, Operations, Project Management, Resource Management, Workflow & Automations
Website: https://asana.com/
