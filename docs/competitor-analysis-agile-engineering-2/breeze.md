# Breeze

1. Question: Solves “simple, low-process-overhead” project management and lightweight product planning (ideas/backlog/roadmaps) for teams that want to ship without complexity; targets product teams plus cross-functional collaborators (engineering/design/stakeholders).
2. Question: Yes. Explicit “product management software” positioning (idea validation, launch planning, stakeholder alignment) while keeping it usable for the broader delivery team.
3. Question: Roadmap boards designed to keep milestones tied to execution reality; calendar-first visibility via due dates; built-in “lightweight” task taxonomy (tags) for rapid organization; templates for product backlog and product development workflows; emphasis on fast onboarding and low learning curve.
Description: Breeze.pm is a simple, visual project management tool designed for teams to plan, track, and collaborate without unnecessary complexity. It features drag-and-drop Kanban-style project boards, task lists, time tracking, reporting, and built-in team chat. It is used by various industries, including marketers, designers, and developers.
Notes:   • Not specifically focused on Product Management
  • Sells itself as a general management tool for every situation (one-solutions-fits-all kind of product)
PM: No
Solutions: Accountants, Attorneys, Church & community, Construction, Design, Developers, Event project management, Finance, Freelancers, HR, Managers, Marketing, Non-profits, Operations teams, Product Management, Property management, Real estate, Remote work, Sales
