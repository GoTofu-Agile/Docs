# DoveTail

1. Question: Solves turning qualitative feedback into actionable intelligence: centralize feedback, auto-classify into themes, and track trends/impact; targets teams doing research and VoC synthesis (product, UX, design, CX/CS, insights).
2. Question: No. Purpose-built around customer intelligence/research repository workflows rather than PM planning objects—used heavily by researchers/designers/insights teams, and then consumed by PMs for direction-setting.
3. Question: AI Channels that auto-classify raw feedback into themes (with added context to steer categorization); dashboards combining quant metrics (e.g., NPS/CSAT) with qualitative themes/sentiment; AI-generated summaries/reports/reels for “stakeholder-ready” storytelling; automated import and theme analysis of G2 reviews into channels.
Description: Dovetail is an AI-native customer intelligence platform that centralizes, analyzes, and synthesizes qualitative customer feedback—such as interview recordings, support tickets, and surveys—into actionable insights. It uses AI for auto-transcription, sentiment analysis, tagging, and creating, summarizing, and sharing research reports.
Notes:   • Not specifically focused on Product Management
  • Mainly a tool for analysis of customer feedback but not an integrated management tool

PM: No
Solutions: AI Agents, AI Analysis, AI Chat and Search, AI Dashboards, AI Docs, API and Integrations
