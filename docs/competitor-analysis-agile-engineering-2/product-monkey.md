# Product Monkey

1. Question: Solves design-to-delivery handoff pain by converting wireframes/Figma screens into draft PRDs and engineering tickets (user stories, acceptance criteria, test/edge cases); targets PMs plus product/engineering teams that want faster spec + ticket creation.
2. Question: Mostly yes. It explicitly references product requirements and PM productivity, but positioning is narrower than a full PM suite: focused on documentation/ticket generation rather than strategy/roadmap/feedback management.
3. Question: Differentiators: image/Figma URL input, dual output (PRDs + tickets), customizable ticket sections, optional user-flow/additional-instruction prompts, and “generate from my titles”; privacy-forward claims (data not used for model training, deletion options), editable/shareable drafts, and low-cost usage-based tiers. Current workflow is copy/paste into Jira/Asana/Trello; Jira/Figma plugins are stated as in development.
PM: No
