# ProductBoard

1. Question: Solves scaling product decision-making: consolidate customer needs, prioritize what to build, and align stakeholders around a roadmap; targets product managers/leaders and cross-functional partners in larger orgs (“built for scale”).
2. Question: Yes. Explicitly “purpose-built for product management,” emphasizing feature ideas linked to objectives/initiatives/goals and customer-centric prioritization. 
3. Question: Roadmaps backed by persistent customer insights; AI feedback categorization + AI search that links insights to feature ideas; structured integrations/open APIs and delivery-tool sync (e.g., Atlassian Jira); ingestion of external review sources such as G2 for competitive analysis.
Description: Productboard is a cloud-based, customer-centric product management platform designed to help teams understand user needs, prioritize what to build next, and align stakeholders on a shared product roadmap. It serves as a central repository for feedback, offering tools for prioritization (e.g., matrices), roadmapping, and AI-powered insights.
