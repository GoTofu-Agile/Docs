## Primary customer group: Scale-ups / SMB product companies with existing customer research data

**Typical roles**

- Product Manager / Senior Product Manager
- UX Researcher / User Research Lead
- Head of Product in smaller teams

**Why them**

- They already sit on **interview transcripts, surveys, support tickets, CRM notes, or call logs**
- They need to validate ideas faster without running full interview cycles every time
- They are close enough to day-to-day decisions to feel the pain immediately and adopt an MVP quickly

**Main pain points**

- Customer interviews are slow, expensive, and hard to repeat at scale
- Existing research data is underused and scattered across tools
- Teams need faster feedback on new features, messaging, and experiments
- Research capacity is limited, so many decisions are made with weak customer input
- Hard to test multiple hypotheses or segments quickly

**Core value proposition**

- Upload existing customer data and generate synthetic personas/interview groups
- Run faster idea validation, concept testing, and lightweight feedback loops
- Scale research output without scaling manual interview effort

**How we can reach them**

- Warm outreach to product managers / heads of product in startups and scale-ups
- LinkedIn outreach with concrete use case: “turn past customer interviews into synthetic test groups”
- Founder / PM communities, startup accelerators, university founder networks
- Product and UX communities on Slack, Reddit, Discord, and LinkedIn
- Demo-led outreach using sample workflows: feature test, message test, persona simulation

## Backup customer group: B2B SaaS founders / startup teams with some customer data

**Typical roles**

- Founder / Co-Founder
- Early Product Lead
- Growth Lead / GTM Lead

**Why them**

- They have strong need for fast validation
- They often lack a dedicated research team
- They can make fast buying / pilot decisions if the value is obvious
- Best fit when they already have at least some usable customer data

**Main pain points**

- Too little time to run structured discovery continuously
- Small teams must make product and messaging decisions fast
- Customer feedback exists, but not in a usable system
- Hard to test positioning, onboarding, or feature ideas before building
- They want a scalable alternative to repeatedly interviewing the same few customers

**Core value proposition**

- Convert existing customer knowledge into reusable synthetic personas
- Use those personas for rapid validation of product, onboarding, and messaging ideas
- Reduce dependency on constant live interviews

**How we can reach them**

- Direct founder outreach through LinkedIn and personal networks
- Startup hubs, incubators, and entrepreneurship communities
- Demo in class / university / founder circles to get pilot users
- Content showing “how to validate ideas faster with customer data you already have”
- Outreach to teams already using surveys, CRM notes, or support transcripts
